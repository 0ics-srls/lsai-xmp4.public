How it works

Three calls. Done.

01
Search a symbol
Find a class, function, or method by name in any indexed library.
xmp4_search(
  "tokio/tokio", "spawn")
02
See the callers
Get type-resolved call sites. Not grep. Not approximate.
xmp4_callers(
  "tokio::task::spawn")
03
Get the source
Full method body + signature, ready to reason about.
xmp4_source(
  "tokio::task::spawn")
